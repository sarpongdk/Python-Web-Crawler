About Tracy and Thom

Tracy and Thom live in Minturn, CO with their 3 dogs, 4 chickens and 1 cat. They love to play in the outdoors hiking, skiing and biking and can do all of these activities just steps from their door. Thom has lived in the valley since he was 7 years old, and Tracy since 2003. Thom works as a contractor and Tracy as a yoga instructor. We are local and available to help you have a great vacation.

Tracy and Thom purchased this House in 2006

Why Tracy and Thom chose Minturn

This apartment is a fabulous retreat from the busyness of the ski resort. It is exciting and fun to go into town and enjoy the mountain and special events and the night scene and entirely peaceful to come home to minturn.
