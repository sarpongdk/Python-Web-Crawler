About Lisa Steele (Owner)

My name is Lisa Steele, and my family and I are the proud owners of a lovingly cared for vacation property at Hanalei Bay Resort on the North Shore of Kauai. I would love the opportunity to share my home away from home with you!

Lisa Steele (Owner) purchased this Condo in 2003

Why Lisa Steele (Owner) chose Princeville

We fell in love with this beautiful, tropical resort during a vacation to Kauai many years ago. It is the ideal image of paradise! We finally had the perfect opportunity to purchase this unit in 2003 and now are able to visit our island paradise several times a year.

What makes this Condo unique

Our happy place is sitting on the lanai enjoying the views and having a meal, reading a book, or just taking in the scenery. The stresses of everyday life just melt away when you are here in paradise!

The very last condo at the very last building at Hanalei Bay Resort puts you squarely in a tropical paradise. Lush jungle surrounds the entrance area across the sidewalk, and an ocean view greets you when you walk in the door. I was impressed with the updating of the unit. The floor, furnishings and bathroom are first class, with plenty of modern tile. This unit is at the bottom of the hill, so it's not as centrally-located as some of the other units at Hanalei Bay Resort. But that can be a good thing, and the resort golf cart will take you there or pick you up when you don't feel like walking up or down the hill. There's a small bar with plenty of dishes, bowls and utensils, and a small refrigerator. A microwave would have been nice but no big deal. Overall a very nice unit I highly recommend.
