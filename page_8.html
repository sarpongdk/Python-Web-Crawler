About Eric Guerra

I'm a wine lover, music lover and I was born to travel the world. In the last year I've visited Italy, Greece, the UK, Mexico, Alaska, Hawaii and and many places in between. Make yourself at home and enjoy my home like I've enjoyed so many other people's homes in cities around the world. I strive for the utmost in hospitality, cleanliness and trust. You'll feel right at home in the middle of Hollywood, the most glamorous city on earth.

Eric Guerra purchased this House in 2001

Why Eric Guerra chose Hollywood

I've lived in Hollywood for 20+ years and have taken immense pride in being a part of this incredible community. My home reflects my love for the magic of Hollywood and my respect for this iconic city. Please respect my neighbors and your surroundings. I've spent many years restoring this beautiful home to its original Hollwood glamour. Please treat my home as if it were your own.

What makes this House unique

Live like a star. You're 4 blocks away from all the action: Hollywood & Highland, Graumann's Chinese Theater, The Kodak Theater and so much more. You're a 5 minute drive to Melrose, The Arclight Theaters, The Hollywood Bowl, Paramount Studios and The Hollywood Sign. You're staying in the heart of Hollywood, and you can see so much by foot including cinemas, gyms, restaurants, stars on the Hollywood Walk of Fame, bars, night clubs, and more. This is the hippest and most vibrant part of the city.
